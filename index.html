About Leonardo

Leonardo Wilhelm DiCaprio is an American actor, film producer, and environmentalist. He has often played unconventional roles, particularly in biopics and period films.

As of 2019, his films have grossed over $7.2 billion worldwide, and he has placed eight times in annual rankings of the world's highest-paid actors.

Beyond his acting career, DiCaprio is a committed environmentalist who has actively worked to raise awareness about climate change and environmental issues through his foundation and various campaigns.

Environmental Advocacy

Leonardo DiCaprio has been a passionate advocate for environmental issues throughout his career. He uses his platform to raise awareness about climate change, biodiversity loss, and other critical environmental challenges.

Through his foundation and personal activism, DiCaprio has supported numerous projects focused on protecting vulnerable ecosystems, implementing innovative solutions to climate change, and empowering communities to become environmental stewards.

He has produced and narrated several documentaries on environmental topics, including "Before the Flood" and "Ice on Fire," which have reached millions of viewers worldwide.
